Privacy Policy for Maths Bearing

Effective Date: 01/01/2025

Last Updated: 01/01/2025

Ming Tsang ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect any information you provide while using the mobile game Maths Bearing ("the Game"). By using the Game, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information, including your name, email address, phone number, location, or device information, as part of the Game's functionality. However, the following user-generated content may be collected:

  • Virtual Names: When you submit your scores to the leaderboard, you may be asked to provide a virtual name. This information is used solely to display rankings on the leaderboard.
  • Scores: Your game scores are recorded in the leaderboard to enhance gameplay functionality.

No cookies or tracking technologies are used to monitor your activities.

2. How We Use Your Information

The user-generated content (virtual names and scores) is collected and stored exclusively to provide and enhance the functionality of the leaderboard. This data:

  • Does not reflect schooling achievements or assessments.
  • Is used only within the Game to improve user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase as a real-time database to manage the leaderboard functionality. Firebase collects and stores your leaderboard data (virtual names and scores) in accordance with its privacy policy. You can review Firebase’s privacy policy here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

No other third-party applications or services collect or access your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your data to any third-party applications, advertisers, or external organizations. The information collected is used solely within the Game for its intended functionality.

5. User Rights

You have the following rights concerning your data:

  • Amendment: You may request changes or corrections to the virtual name or scores you submit to the leaderboard.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your leaderboard data (virtual names and scores).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hmtsang713@gmail.com.

Please note that the Game itself does not provide options to disable ads, as it does not control ad functionality.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Game is targeted at teenagers aged 14 to 18. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at hmtsang713@gmail.com, and we will promptly delete it.

7. Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. User-generated content (virtual names and scores) is stored in a password-encrypted format and is not publicly disclosed through online platforms, including forums or social media.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page without prior notice. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about any updates.
